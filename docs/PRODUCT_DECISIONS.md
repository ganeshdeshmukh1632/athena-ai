# Product Decisions

## PD-001

Athena never executes trades.

---

## PD-002

Athena never gives guaranteed buy/sell calls.

---

## PD-003

Every recommendation must include evidence.

---

## PD-004

Every recommendation must include risks.

---

## PD-005

Every recommendation must include a confidence score.

---

## PD-006

Athena always explains its reasoning.

---

## PD-007

Athena uses multiple AI agents instead of relying on one model.

---

## PD-008

If evidence conflicts, Athena presents the disagreement instead of hiding it.
