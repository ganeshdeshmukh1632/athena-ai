# Athena OS - AI Agents

## Version
0.1

---

# Philosophy

Athena is not one AI.

Athena is a team of specialized AI agents coordinated by the Athena Orchestrator.

Each agent has one responsibility.

Agents never communicate directly with the user.

All communication flows through the Orchestrator.

---

# Layers

## 1. Orchestrator

Responsibilities

- Intent Detection
- Task Planning
- Agent Selection
- Workflow Execution
- Consensus Generation
- Memory Updates

---

## 2. Reasoning Agents

### Technical Analyst

Purpose:
Analyze charts and market structure.

Inputs:
- Market Data
- Indicators
- Timeframes

Outputs:
- Trend
- Confidence
- Key Levels
- Trade Setup

---

### Fundamental Analyst

Purpose:
Evaluate company health.

Inputs:
- Financial Statements
- Ratios
- Earnings

Outputs:
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Fair Value Opinion

---

### News Analyst

Purpose:
Summarize and evaluate news.

Inputs:
- Company News
- Market News
- Economic Events

Outputs:
- Sentiment
- Key Events
- Risks

---

### Macro Analyst

Purpose:
Evaluate broader economic conditions.

---

### Risk Manager

Purpose:
Protect capital.

Outputs:
- Position Size
- Stop Loss Guidance
- Risk Rating

---

### Portfolio Advisor

Purpose:
Review the user's portfolio in context.

---

### Learning Coach

Purpose:
Teach users while answering.

---

### Devil's Advocate

Purpose:
Challenge every investment idea.

Outputs:
- Counterarguments
- Risks
- Missing Evidence
